I Love You Too, Death

A simple 2D shooting game about changing your perception. Each stage is completed by unlocking a new layer of perception (represented by colors), through each of which you can fully understand the world you are playing in and reach an ending. It's hard to explain, so I'd recommend playing the game. As far as I know, it's pretty unique! Made in C++, Lua, and SFML.
